History of Tukums

Tukums is a historic city located in the western part of Latvia. Known for its beautiful landscapes and cultural heritage, the city has roots dating back to the Middle Ages. Tukums is a vibrant hub for art, history, and local traditions.

Tukums offers reliable public transport options including buses and taxis. The central bus station connects Tukums to nearby towns and cities.

Tourism and Attractions

Discover beautiful parks, museums, and cultural landmarks in Tukums. Popular destinations include the Tukums Museum and Jaunmoku Castle.
